Day 3: Melipeuco to Nevados de Sollipulli
Got to Melipeuco at the end of day 2, and after a nice asado and vino set off for the Nevados de Sollpulli with my cousins on day 3. Scenery was spectacular, the company fabulous, the ride rough with lots of hike a bike up monstrous climbs. In other words - perfect! At the top of the Nevados, we were met by my Aunt Edelweis Kucera who had come on horseback from the Reigolil side of the mountain and she´ll guide me over the pass to the Maite valley. This is a movingly beautiful place, unspoiled, skirting around a volcano (you can see it in my Ruta albumn), crossing rivers, deep valleys and amazing views. More commentary on the photos themselves.
